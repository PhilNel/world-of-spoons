Here is my amazing pistachio ice cream recipe!

## Ingredients

- 200g pistachios
- 500ml cream
- 100g sugar

## Method

1. Toast the pistachios in a dry pan.
2. Blend with sugar.
3. Combine with cream, churn.
4. Freeze and enjoy!
